Frequently Asked Questions

How to play LastOneLost?

The game features 12 matches arranged in 3 rows of 4 matches. On each turn, take any number of matches from one row. The player who takes the last match loses. Plan your strategy carefully!

Why are there ads in the game?

The ads help support the development and maintenance of the free game. We use Yandex Advertising Network to display non-intrusive banner ads.

Can I play with friends?

Yes! You can play locally with friends on the same device (hot-seat mode) or challenge our smart AI bot for solo practice.

Is internet connection required?

The core game works offline, but ads require internet connection to load. All game features are available without internet.

How does the leaderboard work?

The leaderboard tracks your game statistics locally on your device. You can compare your progress with friends who play on the same device.

Can I remove ads?

Currently, the game is supported through ads and there is no option to remove them. We try to keep ads non-intrusive.

Game not working properly?

Try these steps:

  • Close and restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • Check your internet connection for ads loading
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the game
